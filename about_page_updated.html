Our Story

Founded in 2009 by Samarendra Jagdev, Pure Space Studio is a Bhubaneswar-based interior design firm committed to creating spaces that blend simplicity with luxury. Over the years, we have successfully completed 100+ residential and commercial projects, making us a trusted name in modern interior design.

We are pioneers in modular kitchen and pool kitchen designs, delivering functional, stylish, and custom solutions that cater to the unique needs of every client. From concept to completion, our focus remains on innovation, sustainability, and precision craftsmanship.

Our Vision

To redefine everyday living by designing interiors that inspire comfort, creativity, and elegance while keeping simplicity at the heart of every creation.

Our Mission

To create meaningful spaces through thoughtful design, premium materials, and personalized client experiences.
